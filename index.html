About Me!

Hi! My name is Mercedes Casler

Right now, I'm completing the Data Analytics Program for Merit America and learning Python on the side.
Previously, I used data analytics to assist me in my role as a Store Manager for adultmart and as a Department Manager (Sporting Goods) for Wal-Mart. Both jobs required me to analyze past sales trends to make decisions revolving around product ordering for new features and lines. Further, I had to analyze previous sales data to make decisions about hiring, firing, and retraining for adultmart.
Due to my extensive background with data analysis at those past jobs, I know that I will thrive in a data analyst field. Especially since my knack for identifying sales trends led to all locations I managed seeing an increase in sales and profit even when my counterparts did not. I look forward to getting promoted to a Senior Data Analyst position!
